Questions

Frequently Asked

Can I change plans at any time? +

Yes. You can upgrade or adjust your plan at the end of each monthly cycle, no penalties. Just let us know at least 15 days in advance.

Do the plans have a minimum commitment period? +

The minimum commitment is defined in the contract. However, the full semi-annual payment requires completing all 6 months, except under the terms stipulated in the agreement.

What if my brand needs something that isn't in the plan? +

We can quote additional services: extra photography sessions, one-off campaigns, web design, and more. Each plan is a starting point, not a ceiling.

Is the advertising budget included? +

Paid media management and strategy is included. The advertising budget itself (the money that goes directly to Meta, Google, TikTok, etc.) is defined and funded by the client separately.

How long does it take to see results? +

The first 30 days are focused on immersion and strategy. From the second to the third month, strategies start generating real results. That's why we recommend a minimum 3-month contract.

What exactly does "1 monthly production" include? +

One monthly photo or video production session focused on generating content for social media. The duration and scope are coordinated at the start of each month based on the content plan.
